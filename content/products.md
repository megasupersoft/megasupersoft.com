# Software

## FFFFinance

Local-first personal finance for New Zealand. Bank-connected, AI-powered, 20+ themes.

- **Open Banking** — Connect your NZ bank accounts via Akahu. Real balances, real transactions, auto-categorised.
- **19 Dashboard Panels** — Net worth, spending, cashflow, budget, tax, runway, and more. Drag, resize, make it yours.
- **AI Assistant** — Ask Freddy about your finances in plain language.
- **Local Storage** — Everything stays on your machine. Cloud sync only if you choose.
- **Cross-Platform** — macOS (Apple Silicon + Intel), Windows, Linux.

[Downloads](/downloads) · [FFFFinance Docs](https://ffffinance.org) · [GitHub](https://github.com/DangerDrome/FFFFinance_app)

## OpenComp

An open-source image compositor for humans and VFX artists. Node-based compositing without the complexity tax. Built for artists who want power without the pain.

## NodeUI

A multimedia canvas for creative work. Connect, compose, and transform media in a node-based interface. Think of it as a visual playground for images, video, audio, and data.

## FileUI

A cross-platform file directory app designed for artists and content creators. Browse, preview, and organise your assets the way your brain actually works — not the way your operating system thinks you should.
